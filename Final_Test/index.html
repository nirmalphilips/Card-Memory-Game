HOW TO PLAY

Set Up Your Game:

  • Navigate to the Game Settings tab.
  • Enter your player name and select the number of cards you want to play with.
  • Click Save Settings to begin the game.

Game Objective:

Match all the cards on the board by finding their pairs. The fewer attempts you use, the higher your score will be!

Game Rules:

  • The cards are placed face down. Each card has an identical match hidden on the board.
  • On each turn:
    • Select two cards to flip them face up.
    • If the cards match, they will disappear from the board.
    • If the cards donâ€™t match, they will flip back face down after a brief delay.
  • Scoring System:
    • +20 Points for each correct match.
    • -2 Points for each incorrect match.
  • Your live score will update as you play.

Scoreboard & High Scores:

  • Track your live score and see your highest score displayed during the game.
  • At the end of the game, view the scoreboard to evaluate your performance and compare your best scores.

Start Over Anytime:

Click the New Game button to reshuffle the cards and start fresh with new challenges.

Tips for Success:

  • Take your time to memorize card positions before flipping.
  • Plan your moves to maximize correct matches and minimize mistakes.
  • Aim for a high score to beat your personal best!
