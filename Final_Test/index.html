How to Play

- The cards are placed face down. Each card has a matching pair.
- For each turn, the player selects two cards.
- If the cards match, they are removed from the board.
- If the cards don't match, they are turned face down again.
- At the end of the game, the player receives a score based on the number of correct matches.
- For each correct match you will get 10 Points & For each wrong match 2 Points will be deducted.
